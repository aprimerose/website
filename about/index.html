Aprimerose builds software for the parts of private equity that off-the-shelf tools don't reach: deal intelligence, LP relationship management, and the compliance infrastructure that sits underneath both.

The thesis is simple. Firms that own their data infrastructure compound faster than those that rent it. Every query, every rejection, every outcome that stays inside your systems is a signal your competitors don't have access to.

The confidential compute work — local-inference infrastructure for European companies that cannot route sensitive data through a public cloud — is the natural extension of that thesis to the AI era. The same sovereignty argument, applied to inference.
